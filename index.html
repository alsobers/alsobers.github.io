Introduction

Yummiest Chocolate cake ever!!!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 C. butter, softened
  • 1/2 C. sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Eggs
  • 3/4 C. baking cocoa (unsweetened)
  • 2 C. flour
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 C. Milk
  • 1 C. strong coffee (cooled)
  • t tsp. vanilla extract

The Perfect Chocolate Cake

  1. Sift dry ingredients together; set aside.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy; beat in eggs 1 at a time.
  3. Add sifted dry ingredients alternately with mild, beat gently until combined.
  4. Add coffee and vanilla and stir until well blended.
  5. Bake in 2 greased and floured cake pans at 350 degrees F until cake tests done with a toothpick in the center. About 20 minutes after starting baking.
  6. Cool and remove layers from pans. Complete cooling on cake racks.

Dark Chocolate Fudge Icing

  • 1/2 C.butter melted or Fleischman's unsalted margarine
  • 1 box confectioner's sugar
  • 2 Tbl.unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 tsp. vanila
  • 1 tsp. milk

Melt butter in saucepan; remove from heat and stir in sugar and cocoa, than vanilla and milk.

Stir until glossy and thick

Cool slightly and spread on cake.

Variations

Substitute butter cream frosting for chocolate icing, and decorate cake top with chocolate curls or sprinkles.

Split layers into two thinner layers each after cake is thoroughly cooled. Spread sweetened whipped cream filling between all the layers , then frost sides and top with butter cream frosting or chocolate icing.

If using whipped cream filling, add 1 tsp. mint extract while whipping.

Chocolate mint delight - YUM!
